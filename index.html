About

Hello! I'm Nirmal and thank you for visiting my website.
I'm a third year undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at the University Of Waterloo.

Apart from my academic work, I like coding, playing football (aka. soccer), photography, relaxing with family/friends, reflecting on the Holy Bible, playing video games (especially FIFA on XBOX/PS) and last but not the least, following the English Premier League (Go Chelsea FC !!!)

Coding helps me to think, reason, and develop the logic to solve a given problem. I practice coding by solving various questions/puzzles (ex. hackerrank) and by working on my academic/small personal projects.

I started following photography in August 2012 using Nikon D200 (yes... it is an old one) and have been using it ever since. I'm just playing around with the camera to capture the perfect picture. In order to share my work in photography, I use flickr, the online photo sharing website. So, feel free to take a look at my picture collection and comment about it. :)
